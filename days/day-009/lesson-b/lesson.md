## Day 009 — Lesson B (Applied)

### Scenario: Weekly content engagement snapshot

You are helping a small **content platform** summarize engagement metrics
for a set of articles or posts over a week.

They export for each article:
`{ title, views, likes, tags: [...] }`.

Today you will:

- Reuse Week 1 skills to build a simple engagement summary.
- Practice deciding what matters for a quick “health check” view.

### Concepts in play

- Counting and summing over arrays of objects.
- Basic tag frequency counting.
- Choosing a minimal but useful summary shape.

### What you’ll implement in the exercise

- Helpers that:
  - Compute total views across all articles.
  - Compute average likes per article.
  - Build a frequency map of tags across all articles.\*\*\*
