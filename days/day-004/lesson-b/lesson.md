## Day 004 — Lesson B (Applied)

### Scenario: Basic customer list tools

You are helping a small **subscription service** keep track of its customers.
They export customer data as an array of objects with `id`, `firstName`,
`lastName`, `isActive`, and `plan` fields.

Today you will:

- Apply your **arrays-of-objects** skills to a realistic-feeling dataset.
- Build small helpers that a real team could use in an internal script.

### Concepts in play

- Filtering objects by boolean fields (`isActive`).
- Deriving display values (full names).
- Simple lookups by id.

### What you’ll implement in the exercise

- A function to list customer full names.
- A function to filter active customers.
- A function to find a customer by `id`.

You will reuse patterns from Lesson A (loops + object property access) and
sanity-check results with `console.log`.***
